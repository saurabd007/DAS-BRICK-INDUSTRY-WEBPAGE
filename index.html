About DAS BRICK

Trusted red brick manufacturer from Barpeta, Assam

Building Stronger Foundations Since early 2000s Founded as a family-run kiln in Bhatkuchi, Barpeta, DAS BRICK Industry has been a cornerstone of quality construction in Assam for over two decades. Our mission is simple: to manufacture durable, high-strength red bricks that architects, builders, and homeowners can trust. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern, controlled firing techniques to ensure every brick is uniform in size, color, and quality. When you choose our bricks, you're not just getting a product; you're getting a legacy of reliability and a foundation built to last.We utilize the advanced Hawa Bhatta technique, which uses electric fans to increase the burning efficiency of coal, resulting in a more uniform and stronger brick.
